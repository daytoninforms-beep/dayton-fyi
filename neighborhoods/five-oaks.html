Contribute to Five Oaks

Dayton.FYI is built by the community. Everything on this page comes from residents, public records, and community partners. Here’s how to add your voice.
Share something happening in Five Oaks
Saw something on a neighborhood Facebook group or Nextdoor worth sharing more broadly? Heard about a development, a business opening, a community concern? Tell us in your own words and we’ll verify and include it in the digest.
Verified by email. We’ll credit you by name or publish anonymously — your choice.
Become a Five Oaks correspondent
Are you already active in Five Oaks community groups online? Help bridge the gap between social media and the public record. Correspondents regularly share relevant community updates from Facebook groups, Nextdoor, and other channels — in their own words. No formal commitment required. Learn more →
Submit a community event
Block parties, church suppers, cleanups, yard sales, school plays, food distributions — anything the neighborhood should know about.
Verified by email. Events appear in the Five Oaks digest and calendar.
Submit a community milestone
Births, graduations, marriages, retirements, military service, community achievements, business openings, and obituaries. Free, permanent, and community-owned.
Verified by email. Submitting on behalf of someone else? That’s welcome — just let us know.
Report a neighborhood issue
Potholes, streetlights out, illegal dumping, a concern you raised at a meeting that hasn’t been addressed. Issues are published on the Five Oaks page and tracked publicly.
Verified by email. We also recommend filing with Dayton Delivers for city follow-up.
