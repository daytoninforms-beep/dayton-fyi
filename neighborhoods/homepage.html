Property investor purchases concentrated in three west-side neighborhoods
The Cleveland Federal Reserve has documented rising investor share of single-family purchases in Dayton-area neighborhoods, with cash transactions and concentration in lower-income communities. The specific county-level breakdown is a data integration target for Dayton.FYI.
