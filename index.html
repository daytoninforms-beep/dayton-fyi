Your community. Your information. Free.

Dayton Informs is a free civic information platform built for the people of the Dayton area. We publish community milestones, track public meetings, and make public records accessible — because this information belongs to you.

Community Milestones — obituaries, births, weddings, graduations, retirements, and more. The life announcements that newspapers used to publish, available free to every family in Dayton.

Public Records Explorer — city contracts, property transfers, and building permits in a searchable, neighborhood-organized format.

Dayton Informs is a prototype of the proposed Dayton Community Information District — a publicly-funded, editorially independent local information utility for the city of Dayton.
