Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

Newsletter signup: If you sign up for our email newsletter, we collect your email address. It is stored by our email provider (Substack) and used only to send you updates about Dayton Informs and the Community Information District initiative.

Obituary and announcement submissions: When you submit an obituary or community announcement, we collect the submitter's name and email address. This information is not published. It is used only to contact you about your submission (e.g., to confirm publication or request clarification).

Published content: The content of obituaries and community announcements (name of the person, dates, remembrance text, etc.) is published publicly on this website. Submitters consent to this when they submit the form.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information to anyone.
  • We do not use advertising or advertising trackers on this site.
  • We do not use analytics tools that track individual users across websites.
  • We do not require you to create an account to use this site.

Cookies

This website does not set any cookies. Our email provider (Substack) may set cookies on their own domain if you interact with the newsletter signup.

Data removal

If you are the submitter of an obituary or announcement and wish to have it removed or corrected, email info@daytonfyi.com and we will process your request promptly.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy: info@daytonfyi.com
